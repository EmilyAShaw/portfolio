    P5.JS

    World Clock

    Abstract clock, with universe symbolising time. Also has biometric and body information based on the time of your 'body clock". The sun holds the numerical time, the world travels arond the sun once per day. The moon travels around the earth once an hour. Every minute the asteroid circles the 'solar system'.

    I made this in my second year of Creative Coding.

    Big Bang Simulation

    interactive 'simulation' of 'the big bang'. Hydrogens fuse together when they collide and make helium. Trace elements like lithium and beryllium are also simulated. The user can mouse over to speed up the fusion - or can use the speed function.

    I made this in my second year of Creative Coding.

    Glyph Simulator

    movable glyphs, where the user can shift the color sliders - the glyphs change based on hue saturation and brightness. Each combination creates a unique glyph.

    I made this in my second year of Creative Coding.
